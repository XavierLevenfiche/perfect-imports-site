01 — What we do

Land it, store it, and ship it — with duty timed to withdrawal.

We operate out of a CBP-bonded warehouse in Fort Lauderdale and handle the logistics around it, so your inventory lands, is entered into bonded storage, and ships when you need it.

A

Bonded, duty-deferred warehousing

Bonded, climate-controlled warehousing in Fort Lauderdale with third-party alcohol-licensed handling. Eligible goods store duty-deferred in CBP bonded premises (19 CFR Part 19) — duties are generally paid when goods are withdrawn for U.S. consumption, not all up front at landing.

B

Import 3PL & distribution

Pallet-in / pallet-out with racking, FDA food-facility registered, and climate-controlled. We help importers land, store, and move inventory into U.S. retail and foodservice — with one operator who answers directly.

We also work with PerfectTed matcha (featured on Dragons’ Den, the UK’s answer to Shark Tank) — ask us about US matcha and hojicha sourcing introductions.
