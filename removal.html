Data & Intelligence

Request removal from our datasets

Perfect Imports publishes company-level business data only — company name, website, category, city/state, and a public business role inbox (such as info@ or wholesale@). We do not publish personal contacts: no individual names, personal emails, phone numbers, or social profiles.

If your company would prefer not to appear in our current or future datasets, we’ll remove it — no reason required.

How to request removal

Email us from any address and include:

  1. Your company name
  2. Your company website or the role inbox in question

We’ll confirm and suppress your company, typically within a few business days.

Email froy@perfect-imports.com

We use removal requests only to suppress your company from future dataset builds. The information you send is never used for enrichment, outreach, or marketing, and is not resold. Your company is added to a permanent suppression list that our pipeline checks on every build.
